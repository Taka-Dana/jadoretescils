Lash Care

Do Not...

  • wet your lashes for the first 24 hours
  • go into steam rooms or hot tubs
  • pick/pull/touch extensions
  • touch your eyes/lashes with unwashed hands
  • expose extensions to extreme heat from oven/stove top/intense hot showers
  • sleep on the extensions
  • wear mascara/gel liner/waterproof products

Do...

  • wash extensions every night
  • if makeup worn, wash extensions 3x a week
  • if no makeup worn, wasj extensions after sweat inducing activities, pat dry and brush after cleaning lashes
  • substitute gel/pencil eyeliner with black powder or liquid liner
  • sleep on your hand or on your back
  • brush daily or as needeed
  • book refills every 2-4 weeks or accordingly
